Raag Personalities

The word Raag is a Sanskrit word that literally translates “Love”. But somehow its conventional meaning transformed to “mood”, or “atmosphere”. So in Hindustani music, raag means a melodious scheme of a set of specific notes which creates a particular mood or atmosphere.
