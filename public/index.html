Raag Personalities

The word “Raag” is a Sanskrit word that literally translates “Love”. But somehow its conventional meaning transformed to “mood”, or “atmostphere”. So, in Hindustani music “Raag” means a melodious scheme of a set of specific some notes which creates a particular mood or atmosphere.
