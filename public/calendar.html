Shyamrao Kulkarni Live

March 10th 2018 1:30pm

Finnish Hall, Berkeley

The Shyamrao Foundation is excited to invite you to join veteran singer and musicologist, Pundit Shyamrao Kulkarni, for an afternoon of Raagdaari music on Saturday, March 10 at 1:30pm at the Finnish Hall in Berkeley, CA. Shyamraoji has been singing for over 60 years and has risen to new heights as both master and innovator of Raagdaari performance and pedagogy.

After spending most of his life learning, performing, and teaching music in India, he relocated to the bay area in 2006, where he has attracted a dedicated student base of Raagdaari music devotees.

While challenging and expanding upon conventional Indian music tradition, Shyamraoji has mastered a cutting edge, new style of Raagdaari vocal performance, which he is eager to share in conjunction with the release of his new instructional book, Raagdaari Music Vol. 1. In this performance, he will be singing a selection of afternoon raags accompanied by Mandar Pimpale on tabla and Andrew Kunz on keyboard. This concert will surely be a delight for both new-comers and veterans of Raagdaari music alike.
